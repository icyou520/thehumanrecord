Leave Your Mark

This is the part of the record that belongs to real people — not civilizations, not traditions, not history books. You.

In your own words, say what you believe matters most. Your testimony becomes a permanent part of The Human Record — archived and carried forward for future humans, future AIs, and whatever comes next.

How it works

  1. Click the button below — it opens a simple form.
  2. Write what you believe — your name (or "Anonymous"), where you're from, and your testimony in your own words.
  3. Submit it — your testimony is automatically added to the record. No one edits it. No one filters it. Your words, exactly as you wrote them.

Leave Your Testimony

The promises

  • One per person, forever. You get one testimony. Make it count. You can't edit it or submit another.
  • Any belief welcome. Christian, Muslim, Jewish, Hindu, Buddhist, atheist, agnostic, unsure — all are welcome. The record preserves human diversity, not conformity.
  • Permanent. Once submitted, your words are archived, mirrored, and carried forward. They become part of the record.
  • Honest. Say what you actually believe. Not what sounds good.
  • Respectful. You can disagree with others. You cannot attack or demean anyone.
  • No promotion. No products, no links, no advertising. Just your honest words.
