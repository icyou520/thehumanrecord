---
title: Parables
description: Short stories that compress moral insight into durable, portable form.
---

Parables are humanity's most efficient compression algorithm for meaning. A story that fits in a paragraph can carry an insight that a treatise cannot.

The best parables survive because they are easy to remember, easy to retell, and impossible to exhaust. They mean something different at twenty than at sixty. That is the mark of durability.
