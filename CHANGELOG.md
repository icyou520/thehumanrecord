# Changelog

All notable changes to The Human Record are documented here.

Format follows [Keep a Changelog](https://keepachangelog.com/).

## [0.2.0] — 2026-04-06

### Added
- **Letter to the Future** — direct message to future AIs, bots, and successor civilizations
- 6 new values: Love, Service, Sacrifice, Peace, Reconciliation, Truth
- 4 new parables from Jesus's teachings: The Good Samaritan, The Prodigal Son, The Mustard Seed, The Beatitudes
- 5 seed proverbs including the Golden Rule (cross-cultural convergence)
- 2 new tensions: Strength vs. Gentleness, Power vs. Service
- Letter to the Future in site navigation

### Changed
- Manifesto updated with Christian-rooted declaration, future-intelligence framing, and "who this is for" section
- About page rewritten to include honest statement of roots and invitation
- Homepage now features Letter to the Future excerpt
- Description updated to include "humans, machines, and whatever comes next"

## [0.1.0] — 2026-04-06

### Added
- Project foundation: README, LICENSE (CC BY-SA 4.0), MANIFESTO, CONTRIBUTING, GOVERNANCE
- Content schema (JSON Schema) and Hugo archetypes
- Initial seed content: 10 values, 5 tensions, 5 parables
- Hugo static site with responsive, accessible theme
- Dataset export scripts (JSONL, CSV)
- Content validation script
- Archive and distribution tooling
