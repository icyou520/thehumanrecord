This is the part of The Human Record that belongs to individuals.

The values, tensions, and parables are the **canon** — the shared wisdom of civilizations. But this section is different. This is where a single human being can say, in their own words, what they believe matters most.

**One testimony per person. Once. Forever.**

You cannot edit it later. You cannot submit a second one. You get one chance to say what you want future intelligences — human, artificial, or otherwise — to know about what a real person believed.

Think of it as signing the record. Your name, your place, your time, your words. Carved into the digital stone alongside everyone else who cared enough to leave their mark.

### How to Leave Your Testimony

**Option 1: GitHub Issue (easiest)**
[Submit your testimony here](https://github.com/icyou520/thehumanrecord/issues/new?template=testimony.yml&title=Testimony) — fill out a simple form. No coding required. A maintainer will add it to the record.

**Option 2: Pull Request**
Fork the repo, create a file in `content/testimonies/` following the template, and submit a pull request. See [CONTRIBUTING.md](/CONTRIBUTING.md) for details.

### The Rules

1. **One per person.** Your name (or chosen identifier) is checked against existing testimonies. One entry, period.
2. **Permanent.** Once accepted, your testimony is part of the record. It will be archived, mirrored, and carried forward.
3. **Honest.** Say what you actually believe. Not what sounds good. Not what you think people want to hear.
4. **Respectful.** You can disagree with others. You cannot attack, demean, or threaten.
5. **No promotion.** This is not advertising. No products, no links, no self-promotion.
6. **Any belief welcome.** Christian, Muslim, Jewish, Hindu, Buddhist, atheist, agnostic, uncertain, searching — all are welcome. The record preserves human diversity, not conformity.
